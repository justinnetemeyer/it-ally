IT Ally Menu

IT Ally Corporation is a woman-owned small business with more than 25 years' experience in creating and maintaining custom software applications for Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA). IT Ally created and currently supports Configuration Management (CM) , Data Management (DM), and Post-Delivery Management (PDM) databases and web interfaces for five ship classes including DDG 51, DDG 1000, LPD 17, LCS, and CVN 78.

IT Ally additionally provides Project Management support for all applications developed on and for the NAVSEA Integrated Environment (IDE) and provides subject matter expertise (SME) to Program Executive Office (PEO) SHIPS Front Office.

During development, testing, and execution, IT Ally ensures all security controls meet or exceed the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) 800-171 requirements as well as applicable Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS) clauses for safeguarding DoD Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI).

IT Ally personnel possess DoD SECRET clearances and we are PMP and Oracle Masters certified. When we are not directly writing software code… we specialize in speaking business to the customer and code to the software engineer.
